Opportunity to partner with India’s Premier Cybersecurity contest and International Conference

InCTF is a national level talent hunt for students with aptitude in cybersecurity. It is designed to identify fresh talent from across India in the area of cybersecurity. It is currently in its 11th edition.

Amrita InCTF aims at upskilling students and professionals in cybersecurity and has a well-defined syllabus, training materials. Amrita InCTF spans for 5 months and has 3 rounds - Learning and training round, elimination round and finals. Only the most motivated students reach the finals.

Currently we are hosting the 11th edition of InCTF. Over the past 5 years, companies such as Cisco, VMware, Amazon, NCIIPC, Netcon, Juniper, TBB, Audius have used InCTF as a platform to recruit and build their cyber workforce. Over the past 10 years, we have trained more than 15,000 students from nearly 150 premier institutions, including IITs and NITs, from 23 states taking part

Hence Amrita InCTF is the perfect recruiting platform which will help your organization to get wide exposure among the student community across India and also for you to get access to highly motivated and trained students whom you can recruit.

The final round of InCTF has an international virtual conference and it is expected to attract 1500 additional registrations.
